## 2. 회사 소개 (웹사이트 인용)

> Volker is a leading expert in the research, development, production, and sales of off-grid inverters, hybrid inverters, microinverters, PCBA boards, energy storage systems, and other innovative products.
>
> The factory, R&D center, and office building cover an area of over 6,000 square meters. Volker's inverters are widely utilized in RVs, ambulances, trucks, construction machinery, solar power generation, residential energy storage, and other applications.
>
> The inverters are exported to Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, the USA, Japan, Pakistan, Mexico, Africa, and other countries and regions.
